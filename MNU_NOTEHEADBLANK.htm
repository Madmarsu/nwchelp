Blank Space Notehead Command

This command selects a blank notehead for use during note entry. When applied to a note, the notehead will be blank, regardless of the note's duration. This can be selected prior to inserting the note, or applied to existing notes on the staff by selecting them and then applying this command. Different noteheads are added in a chord by first selecting the appropriate notehead tool, then adding the note to the chord in the customary fashion.
