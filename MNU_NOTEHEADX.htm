X Notehead Command

This command forces the note to show the X notehead, regardless of its duration. This can be selected prior to inserting the note, or applied to existing notes on the staff by selecting them and then applying this command. Different noteheads are added in a chord by first selecting the appropriate notehead tool, then adding the note to the chord in the customary fashion.
