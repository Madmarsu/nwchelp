Note Properties

Note properties are assigned to one or more selected notes after they have been added to the staff. Note properties include:

  • Extra Accidental Spacing
  • Extra Note Spacing
  • Muted
  • No Leger Lines
  • Slur Direction
  • Tie Direction
  • Lyric Syllable
