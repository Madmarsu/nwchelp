FAQ: Changing the clef

Notes can be changed from one clef to another, as follows:

  1. Select the clef,  press Ctrl+E to change the clef, and change it as necessary.
  2. Press Home, then Shift+End, which selects the whole staff (you can also try this procedure on smaller selections, where appropriate by left clicking and dragging across the notes).
  3. Press Shift+Ctrl+Up/Down Arrow until the notes are at the proper position for the new clef
  4. Press F9 to update the key signature.
