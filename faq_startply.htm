FAQ: Playing notes you just entered

Pressing F5 or Tools, Play will start play back. However, you can set where you want play to begin each time. Select Tools, Options and click on the MIDI tab. Under Play Options, you can select play to start from the beginning, from the current measure, from the insertion point or from several other choices.
