Site License

Site licenses are available for schools, companies and other organizations that would like to install NoteWorthy Composer on more than one computer. The pricing is based on the number of computers on which NoteWorthy Composer will be installed or the number of computers on the network that will use NoteWorthy Composer. Pricing is as follows:

2 to 5 computers #n x US $30

6 or more computers US $150 + [(# - 5) x US $20]

where # is the number of computers at the site to be licensed for use

A 15% discount will be applied to site license orders that total US $250 or more and are accompanied by payment. In other words, prepaid site license orders for 10 or more computers will be discounted by 15%.

Examples of price computation:

A school wants to install NoteWorthy Composer on 8 computers at their site.

US $150 + [(8 - 5) x US $20]= US $210

A company has a server and 16 client computers connected to the server will use NoteWorthy Composer:

US $150 + [(16 - 5) x US $20]= US $370

If the company sends payment with the order, the cost will be US $370 - 15%= US $314.50

For more information on site license purchases, e-mail us at sales@NoteWorthyComposer.com.

Current prices are guaranteed through 31 December 2000. Prices are subject to change thereafter.
