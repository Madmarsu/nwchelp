Ordering by Phone

When ordering by phone, have your credit card information ready, then call 1-888-NTWORTHY (+1-919-557-6757). We accept MasterCard, VISA, American Express, or Discover card. When ordering, please specify that you want to order "NoteWorthy Composer", Item/Product #9611, and have the following information available:

  1. your credit card number
  2. your credit card expiration date
  3. your name exactly as it appears on your credit card
  4. the billing address for your card

Please note: Our telephone line is for orders only. Our operators are not trained to answer your technical questions. Please see the Getting Support section for details on finding assistance with your technical questions.

In addition, your registration codes will not be made available during your call. This information will be included with the product that is shipped to you after your order has been processed.
